WELCOME TO VSMB

Welcome to Vivek Skill Mission Bihar (VSMB)! VSMB, the central hub for computer education, proudly serves as the head office of our institution, and we are pleased to have you here. Located at the heart of Dhansoi, right on the main road near H.N.S.B Janta College, Dhansoi, Buxar, Bihar, India, with a zip code of 802117, our mission is to provide high-quality computer education in the most accessible and friendly manner possible.

At VSMB, we specialize in a variety of computer education programs, which include programming, typing, and ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) courses and more. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated instructors are here to make your journey of improving your computer skills easy and enjoyable. We invite you to explore the opportunities that await you at VSMB. Join us, and let's embark on an exciting learning adventure together. Your success in the world of technology begins here.The Organization's ability to reach the grassroots level, which also offers fantastic placement and growth opportunities, is what makes it special.
